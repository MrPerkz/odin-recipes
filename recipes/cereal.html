Cereal Recipe

Prep Time: 3-5 mins.
Wait Time: As long as you not late.
Serving: Depends on your appetite yeah!
Yield: Advice to use the largest dish available or the bathtub (optional).

Ingredients:

  • Almost any liquid, but preferably Milk...(be it almond, full cream, goat, milk, your mom's, etc)
  • Any brand of cereal
  • Utensil (preferably a spoon or spork)
  • Fruits(optional)

Directions:


  1. Smell and enjoy the aroma of your favourite cereal, while pondering
    whether of not we are alone in the universe or not.

  2. Pour your favourite cereal in the dish/mug/bathtub of your choosing
    (do make sure its clean prior to taking this step).

  3. Pour a CONSUMABLE liquid of your choosing, be it water(spring or still), milk(almon,full cream,goat,etc.), liquor(beer,vodak,etc.) or soda.

  4. Don't allow to simmer, as soggy cereal is not good cereal. Enjoy it immediately for great taste.
Note: Whatever you do never in your life:
POUR THE MILK BEFORE YOU POUR THE CEREAL!
